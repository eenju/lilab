About Us

 Our research group is affiliated with the School of Electronic Science and Engineering, Nanjing University. We are devoted to develop sciences and technologies for post-Moore nanoelectronics and to explore intriguing physical phenomena in condensed matters. Our research interests include nanoelectronics, semiconducotr device physics, synthesis of 2D materials, quntanm transport etc.
 We are constantly seeking highly motivated students and postdocs with background in physics, materials, or electronics. If you are interested in our group, please contact us via sli@nju.edu.cn.
