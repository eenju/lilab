Principal Investigator

Dr. Songlin Li

Associate Professor of Electronic Engineering. He received his bachelor's degree and master's degree from Chongqing University in 2003 and 2006, and his doctor's degree from Institute of Physics, Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2009. From 2009 to 2016, he worked in National Institute of Materials Science in Japan and University of Strasbourg in France, respectively. He published over 80 papers in high-level journals, such as Nat. Commun., Nano Lett., ACS Nano, Adv. Mater. with over 4600 SCI citations and an H index of 30.
